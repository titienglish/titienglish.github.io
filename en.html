Welcome

to T&T ENGLISH

T&T English is on a mission to help children in rural areas, who have limited access to English, develop practical communication skills. We offer 100% free tuition for disadvantaged students, creating a creative and friendly learning environment that enhances their listening, speaking, and natural reflexes. Our curriculum focuses on skills rather than standard classroom textbooks. With the belief that every child deserves the opportunity to learn, we are committed to paving the way for a brighter future.

Welcome

to T&T ENGLISH

T&T English is on a mission to help children in rural areas, who have limited access to English, develop practical communication skills. We offer 100% free tuition for disadvantaged students, creating a creative and friendly learning environment that enhances their listening, speaking, and natural reflexes. Our curriculum focuses on skills rather than standard classroom textbooks. Love and Sharing

T&T ENGLISH is not only a place to learn English but also a place where children are encouraged, loved, and supported to overcome all difficulties.

Quality Education

Our curriculum is scientifically designed, practical, and age-appropriate, helping children develop communication skills naturally.

Equal Development Opportunities

T&T ENGLISH not only teaches English but also inspires children to believe that "there are no limits to knowledge."
